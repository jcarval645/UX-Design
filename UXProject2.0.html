Project Tool Selection Explanation

After evaluating three project management platforms, I decided to proceed with Smartsheet. Even though my project methodology is hybrid iterative rather than predictive, Smartsheet remains the best fit for this project.

One major advantage of choosing Smartsheet is its ability to upload Excel WBS sheets directly, eliminating the time-consuming process of re-entering all tasks manually. It streamlines data import and allows efficient tracking of dependencies and milestones.

Personally, Smartsheet fits my workflow well since I am not naturally organized, and checkboxes alone do not provide enough structure. The platform’s timeline and Gantt chart visualization help me stay on track by viewing the project progress at a glance.
